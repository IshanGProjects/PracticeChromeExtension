Although people love to donate and help others, there are often too many organizations to choose from and many do not use donated funds effectively. RoundUp solves this problem by giving users a few charities from each of the main fields of charities. All of these charities are known for prioritizing change rather than profit or marketing. RoundUp is an easy way to shop guilt free by donating with every-purchase. Most purchases leave behind some spare change, and Round Up encourages you to donate that extra change to a non-profit organization.
